My Story

Born in China, I embarked on a journey that would span continents, cultures, and careers. My pursuit of a Master's Degree in the United States wasn't merely an academic endeavor—it became a transformational experience. Surrounded by a diverse tapestry of individuals, each weaving their own stories, I found myself shedding biases and embracing a mindset that valued the essence of people and ideas over their origins.

After years of exploration to discover my true passion and driving motivation, I've transitioned from the architecture and planning industry to a dynamic realm that provides instant and logical feedback – the world of digital products. Unlike the slow progress of iconic projects like the Sagrada Familia, I now thrive on the rapid evolution of digital solutions.

My journey has led me to immerse myself in the intersection of design and technology, encompassing user experience design, underlying algorithms, and the fascinating world of product management. Experience gained in an education technology firm further fueled my enthusiasm for product management, prompting me to pursue specialized training.

Armed with a unique blend of design and coding skills, complemented by strong organizational and presentation abilities, I am poised to make a significant impact as a product manager.
